Welcome To Gurukul

Gurukul is the center of promoting, reforming and developing the cultural patterns of society which establishes Gurukul School for its own good and welfare in the society. The aim of Gurukul education is self-realization, the fulfillment of human beings instinctive potentialities in order to realize the eternal life. Gurukul always focused on the practice based education which is always useful in the daily life of students. So, the demand of Gurukul education in the society is really high.

To address this demand of society we have established Gurukul School with the name Punyahari Gurukul Ved Vidyashram.

About Us

I thought, I need to do for my late fathers name and grand-mothers name. Today we really need good moral education and all moral education will be guided by sanskrit literature. I decided to open a sanskrit vede vidhyashram at my house where i was born. I grew up in that house. I decided to donate my house to the ved gurukul vidhyashram.

My grand-mothers name is Hari kenya dahal and fathers name is Punya prasad Dahal. So I named the schools name Punya Hari Gurukul ved vidhyashram.

With lot of efforts we have brought our school so far. If you are interested in promotion of school and assist in its development, you are heartily welcome at Gurukul.
